# Math rendering

Inline math is supported using single dollar signs: $E = mc^2$.

Block math uses double dollar signs:

$$
\frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}
$$

You can also write complex matrices:

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
1 & 0 \\
0 & 1
\end{pmatrix}
$$
